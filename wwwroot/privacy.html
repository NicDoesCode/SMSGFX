SMSGFX Privacy

First-party data

While you use the application the following information is created:

  • Your projects into browser local storage.
  • Application settings and preferences into browser local storage.

The above is stored locally within your web browser, it is not transmitted externally or accessible to third parties.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to monitor and analyse website traffic.

Information collected from Google Analytics is used by the authors of this product for the purposes of product improvement, the information is not shared with third parties.

The scope of the information collected is the minimum data set that Google Analytics provides, however it may include the device, operating system and browser type.

In order for the product to work a cookie may be placed on your device.

The information is not personally identifiable.

Google's advertising features are not used.

See "How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps" for information on Google's privacy practices.
